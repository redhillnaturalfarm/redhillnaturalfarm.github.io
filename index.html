Our Story

Red Hill Natural Farm was born from a simple belief: that the best fruits come from healthy soil and sustainable practices. Located in the beautiful countryside, our farm has been practicing natural farming and agroforestry for years.

We don't use synthetic pesticides or chemical fertilizers. Instead, we work with nature, using traditional farming methods combined with modern sustainable techniques like Jeevamrutham (natural fertilizer). Every fruit is hand-picked at peak ripeness to ensure you get the best flavor and nutrition.

We proudly serve Bangalore and Chennai with farm-fresh produce delivered within 24 hours of harvest. When you buy from Red Hill, you're not just getting delicious fruits - you're supporting sustainable agriculture, local farming communities, and a healthier planet for future generations.
